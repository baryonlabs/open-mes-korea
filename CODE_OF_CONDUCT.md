# Code of Conduct

Open MES Korea welcomes contributors from manufacturing, software, operations,
design, translation, research, and implementation backgrounds.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate respectfully and focus discussion on the technical or
  manufacturing problem.
- Explain assumptions, evidence, constraints, and trade-offs.
- Respect differences in language, experience, role, industry, and region.
- Protect factory, customer, worker, and vendor information.
- Accept review and correction without personal attacks.
- Prefer reproducible examples and actionable feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks
- Publishing credentials, customer data, private factory details, or security
  vulnerabilities without permission
- Misrepresenting unimplemented features as production-ready
- Artificial star exchanges, spam, or automated promotional activity
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors for unpaid support

## Manufacturing safety

Contributions involving equipment control, quality disposition, worker safety,
or AI automation must clearly document risks, fail-safe behavior, approval
boundaries, and audit requirements.

## Reporting

For conduct or security-sensitive reports, use the private contact form:

https://openmeskorea.org/#contact

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions and participation
that violate these expectations.
